Notes for IRL Play

Use counters (e.g., coins, tokens) for effects that stack or reset (e.g., Lucky Cat, The Collector). For percentage-based effects (e.g., Lucky Bounce, Phantom Joker), use a d10 die for simplicity. Effects are tuned to avoid runaway scaling (e.g., Seal of Approval capped) and ensure all Jokers are viable. Legendary Jokers are high-impact but situational.
